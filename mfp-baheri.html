Interesting Insights about the Park/SEZ for an investor

  • Equipped with internal road network, boundary wall, streetlight, sanitation
  • Availability of utility services (electricity and dedicated substation and drinking water supply)
  • Bareilly regions is rich in agriculture resources (like sugarcane and rice)
  • Located in the border of UP and Uttarakhand - location suitable for investors from Uttarakhand
  • Rich agriculture resources and food process industrial ecosystem of UP and Uttarakhand – beneficial for investors
  • Nivesh Mitra, Single Window System for online land allotment and other services (24x7)

Incentives Available

Incentives under Industrial Investment and Employment Promotion Policy, 2017:
  • Stamp duty exemption: 75% in Madhyanchal
  • EPF Reimbursement: Facility to the extent of 50% of employer’s contribution to the units providing direct employment to 100 or more unskilled workers
  • SGST Reimbursement: Net SGST reimbursement @90% for Small Industries for 5 years, @60% for Medium Industries for 5 years, @60% for Large Industries other than Mega Industries for 5 years, and @70% for Mega category Industries for 10 years.
  • Capital Interest Subsidy: 5% per annum for 5 years, annual ceiling of INR 50 lakhs
  • Infrastructure Interest Subsidy:5% per annum for 5 years, annual ceiling of INR 1 Crores
  • Industrial Quality Development: 5% per annum for 5 years
  • Electricity Duty: : 100% exemption to all new industrial units set up in the state for 10 years. Also, 100% exemption to all new industrial units producing electricity from captive power plants for self-use for 10 years
  • Mandi Fee: 100% exemption to all new food processing units on purchase of raw material for 5 years.

Incentives under Food Processing Policy of UP:
  • Capital interest subsidy: 25% of Expenditure on P&M up to ₹ 50 lakh; 10% additional for Mega Food Parks
  • Interest Subsidy of Reefer Vehicles: Reimbursement of Interest 7% p.a. for 5 years up to ₹ 50 lakh
  • Preparation of bankable projects: 50% assistance for DPR expense up to ₹ 5 lakh
  • Marketing Development: 50% of cost for transport of samples abroad; 25% of export transportation cost; 20% of F.O.B. Price for export
  • Patent / Design Registration: 75% of fees reimbursement up to ₹ 1.5 lakh
  • Promotion for Standardization: 50% of Fee Reimbursement up to ₹ 50 Lakh for standardization of products
  • Interest Subsidy: 100% of Interest – Micro and Small Units; 7% Interest up to ₹ 50 lakh for 5 years
  • Food Processing Training Centres: 50% of Unit cost up to ₹ 1 lakh
  • Infrastructure Grant-in-Aid: ₹75 lakh for infra facilities for Degree/ Diploma courses in Food Processing
